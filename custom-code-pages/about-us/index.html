About us

Our team has experience working across a wealth of Technology, Delivery, and Hospitality businesses - this includes Uber Eats, Amazon, Deliveroo, and Yo Sushi!, to name just a few.

We bring together a passion for food and for new technologies and our aim is to change the way in which the food delivery sector operates.

Our values

Act like an owner

At PWB each of us acts like we own the business. We never say "that's not my job", we act in the long term interest of the entire business, not for the short term interest of our own team.

Help each other

Take time to help each other, we're bigger than the sum of our parts. Our success is built by the people we work with and taking time to help each other makes us stronger. It's the right thing to do.

Maximise your impact and deliver results

Each of us delivers on our KPIs, and that means hitting the right level of quality and being on time. We’re not busy fools, and we focus on what will have the greatest impact. We consistently rise to the occasion.

Hire and develop the best

Every hire raises the bar, and we promote exceptional talent. We move people willingly and quickly across the business to where they’ll have the biggest impact. We don’t respect ‘industry knowledge’ or ‘connections’, we respect talent and results. We work on ourselves, and we strive to be better each day.
